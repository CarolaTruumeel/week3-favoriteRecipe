Garlic Bread Recipe

Cooking instructions

  • Preheat the oven 200 degrees
  • Prepare the garlic bread
  • Heat in the oven for 10 minutes
  • Sprinkle with parmesan
  • Slice
Ingredients

Italian bread 450g

Unsalted butter 1/2 cup

smashed garlic 2 cloves

parmesan cheese 1/4 cup
